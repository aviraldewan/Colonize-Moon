Tutorial

Rocket Movements

  • Up Arrow: To move rocket up
  • Left Arrow: To move rocket in leftside
  • Right Arrow: To move rocket in rightside

Satelite Movements

  • Up Arrow: To move satelite up
  • Left Arrow: To move satelite in leftside
  • Right Arrow: To move satelite in rightside

Moon Part

Lander Movements

  • Up Arrow: To move lander ahead
  • Left Arrow: To move lander in leftside
  • Right Arrow: To move lander in rightside
  • Down Arrow: To move lander in backside

Rover Movements

  • Up Arrow: To move rover ahead.
  • Left Arrow: To move rover in leftside.
  • Right Arrow: To move rover in rightside.
  • Down Arrow: To move rover in backside.
  • (If Water detected) 'd' key: To deploy Flag.
  • To charge Rover, touch lander.
